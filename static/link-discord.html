Help & Instructions

1
Join our server
Make sure you\'re in Our Discord server
2
Link Discord via OAuth
Launch League of Legends and click the OAuth button on the left to link Discord.
3
Start a match
RiftTalk will react automatically at the moment the game starts.

Important to know:

  • To get into your team channel, you can join the open Waiting Room. You will be moved automatically after match starts.
  • You can manually join your team voice channel in Discord after match starts.
  • You can use the invite link that appears in the app after match starts.
  • If you were disconnected, join Waiting Room (or any voice channel) and click Reconnect in the app.
